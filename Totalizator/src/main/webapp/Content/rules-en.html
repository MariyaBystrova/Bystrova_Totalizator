General rules in totalizator: how to play and to win?

1. General

1.1 Bets are accepted from persons over 18 years of age.

1.2 Bets are accepted only from individuals who agree with these rules. Any bet is a confirmation that the client knows these rules and agree with them. The administration has the right to invalidate the bets made in violation of these Rules. The administration assumes no responsibility to the period when it became known or established subsequently that the bets were made in violation of the Rules.

1.3 The administration has the right not to accept bets from persons not complying with these rules.

1.4 In the case of fraud related to the acceptance of bets or financial transactions, the administration has the right to stop such actions by setting the bets invalid and law enforcement bodies upon fraud.

1.5 Claim on disputed matters shall be made within 10 days after the event. The final decision on all disputes is taken by the administration.

1.6 If the coupon had more than 5 failed matches, this edition is considered to be unresolved, all bets must be refundable.

2. Opening, Deposit and Payout

2.1 First of all, the customer must register in the system. It is necessary to specify first/lastname, patronymic, date of birth, address of residence. In case of inaccurate data supplied, the administration reserves the right to suspend the customer and cancel the bets

2.2 To enhance security it is recommended to periodically change password. You can change your password in the section "Edit account" Settings.

2.3 The client is not entitled to allow third parties to use the account.

2.4 The money will be remitted within 3 working days from the date of the last game of the coupon.

2.5 In cases of incorrectly calculated rates (for example, due to incorrect results), these rates are recalculated. The bets made between the wrong calculation and recalculation, are valid.

2.6 СBets are not accepted from persons who are the participants of the events on which bets are made (athletes, coaches, judges, etc.) and acting on behalf of the participants of the events. Bets are not accepted from persons representing the interests of other totes.

3. General betting rules

3.1 Bets are accepted before the expiration of the coupon specified by the Friday of the current week.

3.2 The minimum bet is determined by the administration. On tote bets, the maximum payout is not limited. The minimum bet amount is determined by the administration specifically for each event.

4. Special calculation rules

4.1 The prize fund - part of the pool (90%), intended for the payment of winnings. The prize fund is divided into four winning categories:

  1. 20% of the pool - in the distribution of that part of the prize fund involved rates, the results of which guessed 15 events;
  2. 15% of the pool - in the distribution includes all bets, which results guessed 15 or 14 events;
  3. 20% of the pool - in the distribution involved all bets, which results guessed 15, 14 or 13 events;
  4. 35% of the pool - in the distribution involved all bets, which results guessed 15, 14, 13 or 12 events.

The more events guessed, the more winning categories it is involved in. The prize pool will be splitted among the winning bets in proportion to the amount of each of them (ie, the higher the bet, the bigger the payout) in each of the categories in which they participate in the distribution.

4.2 Jackpot - additional gain, which is distributed among the bets where guessed the results of 15 matches, in proportion to the value of these bets (ie, the higher the bet, the bigger the payout). It is formed from previous drawings tote which was not played one or more winning categories, i.e. when in the best bet guessed less than 15 (14, 13, 12) events. Jackpot is not being drawn and increases from the drawing to the drawing, till one of the participants does not guess the outcome of all 15 matches.

4.3 The organizer of the totalizator has the right to increase the jackpot from its own funds.

4.4 If the initial result of the completed event is canceled later, for whatever reason, the cancellation is not taken into account, and the rates to be paid in accordance with the initial (actual) result. The actual result is considered to be declared on the basis of the official records and other official sources of information immediately after the end of the event. In the absence of results in the official sources the administration has the right to use alternative sources of information, including information of its own representative at the match.

5. The main types of bets

5.1 Bets are accepted on the actual outcome of the competition. On the first position - for winning the first command ("1"), on the second position - for a draw ("X"), on the third position - the team for winning the second ("2").
